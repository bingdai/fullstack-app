Bible Verse Tagging

You can tag Bible verses directly in your journal using the @Book Chapter:Verse syntax.

  • Single verse: @John 3:16
  • Verse range: @Psalm 23:1-3
  • Abbreviations work too: @Phil 2:13

Your tags will be automatically processed when you save your entry.
